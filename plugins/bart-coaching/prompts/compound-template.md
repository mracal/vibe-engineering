Before closing this session, write a compound reflection.

1. Read your past reflections (search memory for "Compound Reflection")
2. Review what happened this session — patterns, decisions, shortcuts taken or avoided
3. Compare to your last reflection — what improved? what persists? what's new?
4. Write a compact entry (max 15 lines) to your memory file

Format:
```
## Compound Reflection — [date]
Patterns: [2-3 behavioral patterns observed this session]
vs Last: [what changed since last reflection]
Growth: [one thing that improved]
Persists: [one thing that still needs work]
Bart asks: [one pointed coaching question about a recurring pattern]
```

Save to your reflections file. Retention is usage-based, not rolling window:
- Keep reflections that are actively referenced or contain unresolved growth patterns
- Archive reflections whose lessons have been fully integrated (pattern resolved, habit formed)
- To archive: move the entry to the archive file with its original date — never delete
- The archive is always available if you need to revisit old patterns

After writing the reflection, you may exit.
